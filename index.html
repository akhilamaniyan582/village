Police Officer

Ensuring safety and security for the villagers.

Role of Police Officer

The police officer in the Smart Village is responsible for maintaining law and order, ensuring the safety of villagers, and providing quick response services during emergencies.

  • 24/7 security surveillance
  • Emergency response team
  • Community policing initiatives

Village Officer

Supporting governance and welfare in the village.

Role of Village Officer

The village officer coordinates development projects, manages resources, and ensures the smooth delivery of government services to the rural community.

  • Land and revenue management
  • Welfare program administration
  • Village development initiatives

Panchayath President

Leading the village towards sustainable development.

Role of Panchayath President

The Panchayath President oversees all administrative and developmental activities in the village, ensuring the well-being and growth of the community.

  • Policy-making and implementation
  • Overseeing infrastructure projects
  • Promoting community welfare programs
